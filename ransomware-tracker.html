Methodology & Data Sources

Our ransomware tracker aggregates data from multiple open-source intelligence (OSINT) feeds, primarily focusing on data leak sites (DLS) where ransomware groups publish victim data.

Data Aggregation

We monitor 50+ active ransomware leak sites through automated scrapers. Data is cross-referenced with Ransomware.live and other OSINT sources to ensure accuracy.

Updates

The tracker is updated automatically every 4 hours. Victim counts reflect total historically observed victims, while the "7 Days" metric shows recent activity intensity.

Usage

This data is free for researchers and defenders. Use it to track group trends, target industries, and emerging threats. Do not use for negotiation leverage.
