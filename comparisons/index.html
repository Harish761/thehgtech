How We Create Comparisons

A transparent look at our research process

1

Initial Research

We identify the top tools in each category based on market share, analyst rankings, and industry recognition. We typically select 3-5 leading solutions for head-to-head comparison.

2-3 hours Market analysis
2

Review Aggregation

We collect and analyze reviews from multiple platforms: G2 (typically 200-500 reviews per tool), Gartner Peer Insights, TrustRadius, and Capterra. We look for patterns in praise and complaints.

500+ reviews Sentiment analysis
3

Analyst Reports

We reference industry analyst reports including Gartner Magic Quadrant, Forrester Wave, and IDC MarketScape. These provide vendor positioning, strengths/weaknesses, and market direction.

Official reports Vendor rankings
4

Community Research

We dig through Reddit (r/sysadmin, r/cybersecurity, r/netsec), security forums, Stack Overflow, and vendor communities to find real-world user experiences and honest opinions.

Reddit threads Forum discussions
5

Pricing Research

We compile pricing information from official vendor websites, user-reported costs on review platforms, and industry pricing guides. We note when pricing is opaque or requires quotes.

Per-user/endpoint costs TCO analysis
6

Writing & Verification

We synthesize all research into actionable comparisons. Every claim is fact-checked against sources. We clearly distinguish between verified facts and community opinions.

Fact-checked Cited sources
