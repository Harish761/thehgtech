# Splunk vs Elastic Security Comparison - Content Creation Guide

## Article Structure

### 1. Executive Summary
- Quick verdict for different scenarios
- Key differentiators
- Pricing overview

### 2. Methodology Disclosure
**CRITICAL: Be transparent about research approach**
- Based on 500+ G2 reviews
- Gartner Magic Quadrant 2024/2025
- Official vendor documentation
- Community feedback analysis

### 3. Feature Comparison Matrix
Interactive table with:
- SIEM capabilities
- Detection & response
- Integration options
- Deployment models
- Pricing models

## Writing Style
- Professional but accessible
- Data-driven (cite everything)
- Balanced (show pros/cons of both)
- Transparent about limitations
- No fake testing claims

## Sources to Cite
1. Gartner Magic Quadrant 2024/2025
2. G2 user reviews (specific ratings)
3. Vendor documentation
4. Community forums (Reddit, Stack Overflow)
5. Published benchmarks

## What NOT to Say
❌ "In our testing..."
❌ "We deployed both solutions..."
❌ "Our lab environment showed..."
❌ Any claim of hands-on testing

## What TO Say
✅ "According to 500+ G2 reviews..."
✅ "Gartner reports indicate..."
✅ "User feedback suggests..."
✅ "Based on our research..."
✅ "Analysis of community discussions reveals..."
