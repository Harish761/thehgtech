Why US Security Teams Use This Scanner

The Zero-Day Risk Checker is designed specifically for US-based Security Operations Centers (SOCs) and IT administrators. Over 60% of US enterprises use complex, multi-vendor ecosystems. Instead of manually parsing high-noise CTI feeds, infrastructure managers can type their exact tech stack component (e.g., VMware vCenter, F5 BIG-IP) into this interface to instantly retrieve critical CVEs (Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures).


This accelerates emergency patch management, incident prioritization, and helps organizations comply with CISA's Known Exploited Vulnerabilities (KEV) mandates much faster. Results are aggregated from live API feeds targeting high and critical CVSS v3/v4 score incidents.
