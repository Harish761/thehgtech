Audit Reality Check

This engine uses one rapid-fire question per control to measure your baseline readiness. However, during a formal ISO 27001 Certification Audit, external auditors test controls across three phases: 1) Design (Where is your policy documented?), 2) Implementation (What is your exact process?), and 3) Operating Effectiveness (Provide verifiable log samples proving you followed the process). Answering "Yes" here means you are ready to prove all three.
